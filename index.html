About

Hi, I am Jian (James) Wang, currently Research Scientist in Snap Inc. I obtained my PhD degree from Carnegie Mellon University ECE department, advised by Prof. Aswin Sankaranarayanan and Prof. Srinivasa Narasimhan. I'm mainly interested in Computer Vision, Computational Imaging and Computational Photography.
